📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hospital billing data using Microsoft Excel to uncover patterns in treatment costs, patient demographics, medical conditions, and insurance providers. The goal is to support data-driven healthcare decisions through clear insights and visual dashboards.

🎯 Objectives

Identify medical conditions with the highest hospital billing amounts Analyze billing variations across insurance providers Examine how patient age groups impact total billing Evaluate average length of hospital stay by medical condition Compare treatment costs by gender

🗂 Dataset Description

    The dataset contains patient-level hospital records, including:

  • Medical Condition
  • Billing Amount
  • Insurance Provider
  • Gender
  • Admission Date
  • Discharge Date
  • Hospital Name

💡 Key Insights

  • Obesity and Hypertension generate the highest total billing amounts.
  • UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, and Blue Cross contribute significantly to overall billing.
  • Older age groups (especially 73–92) incur higher treatment costs.
  • Average length of stay is relatively consistent across most conditions.
  • Noticeable cost differences exist between male and female patients.

📈 Dashboard Highlights
