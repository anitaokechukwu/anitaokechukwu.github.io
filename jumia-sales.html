📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes 3,000 Jumia customer orders to uncover insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and regional purchasing patterns. Using Power BI, the dataset was cleaned, modeled, and transformed into a fully interactive dashboard that supports decision-making in e-commerce operations.

🎯 Business Problem

Management wanted to understand sales performance, identify high-value customers, determine best-selling products, and improve business decisions using data.

💡 Key Insights

  • Top Regions: East and South regions contribute the highest revenue, indicating strong customer engagement.
  • Best-Selling Categories: Food & Beverages dominate overall sales, followed by Electronics and Home Appliance.
  • High-Value Customers: Doctors and Students generate higher average order values.
  • Income vs Quantity:
  • Mid-income customers (₦40K–₦80K) purchase more items.
  • High-income customers buy fewer but more expensive products.
  • Gender Split: Sales are nearly evenly distributed — Male (49.79%) and Female (50.21%).
  • Age Segments: Customers aged 35–54 make the highest purchase contributions.
